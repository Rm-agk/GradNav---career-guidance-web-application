CAO Website

CAO.ie is an online platform facilitating undergraduate course applications in Ireland, while GradNav serves as a pre-application tool, assisting Leaving Certificate students in exploring suitable courses and career paths based on their interests and skills. Together, they provide a comprehensive approach to educational guidance, with CAO.ie managing the application process and GradNav aiding students in making informed decisions before applying.
